Pizza Recipe

Have you ever craved homemade pizza just like your nonna used to make? Here is a simple recipe to follow to enjoy that same love your nonna used to make.

First the Ingredients:

  • cheese, preferabley mozzarella!
  • pizza dough
  • the sauce, choose your favorite, but using fresh crused tomatoes is the best!
  • any toppings you like such as pepperoni or mushrooms

5 Simple Steps:

  1. Roll out your pizza dough into thin little pizzas using your hands or a rolling pin
  2. To banke the pizza, preheat the oven to to 450 degrees
  3. Place your rolled dough, onto a cooking sheet. Add your sauce first, then cheese and any toppings.
  4. Put in the oven and cook for about 15 minutes or until the crust is a golden brown.
  5. Let it sit for about 5 to 8 mins to cool.

There you have it! Your home made pizza is ready for you to enjoy, just like nonna used to make.
